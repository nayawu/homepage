About us

What is Nayawu?
Nayawu is a project created by band of passionates. We love to listen, play and record literally everything around us. We are proud to share our music with the rest of the world.

How does it sound?

Our sound is based around hypnotic polyrhytmical structures and developing harmonical layered textures inspired by psychedelic culture(both live and electronic), dub, chillout and ambient.
